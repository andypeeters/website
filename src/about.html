About me

My name is Andy Peeters. I work as a .NET C# software developer for a small company called Ometa, a data integrator and solutions provider. On this blog, I write articles and opinions about everythings that interests me, ranging from tech (software and hardware), over anything science related, to even language learning and Japanese classical martial arts. Perhaps I'm a bit of a nerd, I guess.

One note: this blog, its contents and opinions are my own and do not represent my employer's view in any way.

At the same time, I'm regularly feel being the biggest imposter and phony living on this planet. Probably a curse on each and every person who attempts to build stuff and move forward in life.

Feel free to subscribe to my RSS feed, follow me on Mastodon or checkout some code on my Github profile.

Background

Started working in tech on August 2001 for Atos, then called Atos Origin. After about 14 years it was time for a change and I moved on to the IT department of the Antwerp University Hospital. Although it certainly was a positive experience, something kept itching and 2 and a half years later I left to join Ometa.
